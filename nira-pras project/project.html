Shree Harikul Facilities

At Shree Harikul, we believe that a stimulating learning environment is the foundation of quality education. Our campus provides safe, modern, and well-maintained facilities that support every aspect of a student’s development. Our science and computer laboratories give students hands-on experience with modern tools and technology. The library and reading hall offer a quiet space for research and self-study, while our smart classrooms make learning interactive and engaging. Outside the classroom, students enjoy a spacious playground for sports and games, a hygienic canteen for healthy meals, and reliable school transportation for safe travel. For the youngest learners, our Montessori rooms are colourful, creative spaces where curiosity thrives. We also have a first-aid and medical room, ensuring the safety and wellbeing of all our students, and a multi-purpose hall for cultural programs, competitions, and school gatherings. At Harikul, we continuously upgrade our facilities to keep place with modern educational standards and to nurture every student’s physical, mental, and creative growth.
